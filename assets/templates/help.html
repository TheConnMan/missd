Using Miss.d

Miss.d is used to send you alerts when regularly scheduled jobs don't trigger after a certain period of time. Each job you create comes with an API key which your job should POST to after completion. If your job does not POST to Miss.d within the expiration time period your job notifications are sent out.

Quick Start

  1. Create a Miss.d job
  2. Create notifications for your job
  3. Send a POST to {{ingestUrl('your-api-key')}} after each job completion
  4. Don't worry about your job until you get a notification!

Force Expiration

To force expire a job (e.g. a job is in a known bad state) send a POST to {{expireUrl('your-api-key')}}.
